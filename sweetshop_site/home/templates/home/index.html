About

Our little shop right in the heart of Corfe Castle and next to the castle ruins, is a 17th century building. There is a date stone on the front of the building depicting the actual year.

Sweets

Sugar/Gluten Free

We stock a fine selection of sugar free and gluten free sweets, chocolates and biscuits. Some using the sugar substitute Stevia which is a natural sweetener.

Liqourice

We are a main stockist of Liqourice of all kinds, salt, double salt, Dutch, Italian, soft, hard, and gluten free as well as Natural Liquorice root. Liqourice, as well as being delicious has many medicinal uses.

Traditional Sweets

Travel back to your childhood and choose your favourite, Sherbet lemons, pips, cola cubes, bon bons we have them all.

Retro

Sherbet Fountain, Dib Dab, Fruit Salad, Black Jacks, Refreshers, Love Hearts. to name but a few of the sweets we all remember.
